The Dev Note

The Dev Note is a wiki for IT professionals, enthusiasts, and prosumers. It is a wealth of knowledge featuring process guides, how-to's and best practices.

The project began during the pandemic as Sevan Learning, it was pulled due to lack of content. It has been under development for the past 4 years and will be launching later this year as The Dev Note Wiki. It is not yet available for public preview, but will be coming in the next few months.

FreedomRadio

FreedomRadio is the oldest remaining project from Sevan/World of Sevan. It is primarily a playlist driven network of music on Apple Music. Playlists range from Rock to Hip-Hop and in between. It is constantly being updated, so be sure to add it to your collection.
